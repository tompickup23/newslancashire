Three schools in Hyndburn received lower ratings following Ofsted inspections in spring 2026.

Accrington Academy dropped from Good to Requires Improvement, with inspectors citing inconsistent teaching quality and below-average pupil progress in mathematics.

Two primary schools in Great Harwood also received lower ratings. The inspections form part of Ofsted's routine cycle of school assessments.

Hyndburn Borough Council's education portfolio holder said the authority was "working closely with school leadership teams to address the areas identified for improvement."

Sources: Ofsted inspection reports, March-April 2026.
