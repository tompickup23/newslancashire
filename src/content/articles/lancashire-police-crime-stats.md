Lancashire Constabulary recorded 14,200 violent offences in the 12 months to March 2026, according to the latest crime statistics. That is a 12% rise on the 12,678 offences recorded in the previous year.

Burnley saw violent crime rise by 18%, while Blackburn with Darwen recorded a 16% increase. Lancaster was the only borough to see a reduction, with a 3% fall.

The figures come as the Police and Crime Commissioner faces questions over the allocation of neighbourhood policing resources across the county.

Sources: Lancashire Constabulary recorded crime statistics Q4 2025-26.
