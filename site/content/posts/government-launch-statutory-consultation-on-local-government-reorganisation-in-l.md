The statutory consultation, launched by the Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government (MHCLG), runs from 5 February to 26 March 2026 and invites views on proposals to reorganise local government across Lancashire. This is the last chance for people to share their views on the future of local government structures in Lancashire.
