About News Lancashire

News Lancashire is Lancashire's premier local news source, bringing together stories from every corner of the county into one fast, clean, personalised feed.

What We Do

We aggregate news from trusted local and national sources covering Lancashire, including BBC Lancashire, Lancashire Telegraph, Burnley Express, Blackpool Gazette, Lancashire Evening Post, and more. We also monitor social media posts from Lancashire MPs and public figures on Bluesky.

AI-Enhanced Content

Every article on News Lancashire features an AI-generated summary that provides a concise, original overview of the story with a local angle. High-interest stories receive additional AI analysis providing context and perspective. We believe in transparency about our use of AI technology.

Our Approach

  • Factual and objective — We present the news as it is, without sensationalism
  • Local first — Every story is framed through a Lancashire lens
  • All 14 boroughs — From Burnley to Blackpool, Lancaster to Chorley
  • Always free — No paywalls, no subscriptions, no ads
  • Fast — Updated every 30 minutes with the latest stories

Source Attribution

We always credit the original source of every article and provide a direct link to the original story. We encourage readers to visit the original sources for full coverage.

Contact

News Lancashire is published by Tom Pickup. For enquiries, corrections, or to request removal of content, please visit our contact page.

Technical

News Lancashire is built with Astro, runs on free infrastructure, and is designed to be the fastest local news site in the UK. Page loads are typically under 100ms.
